STARBUCKS CUSTOMER SERVICE POLICY
Version 4.2 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Applies To: All Store Partners and Shift Supervisors

================================================================================
SECTION 1: CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE STANDARDS
================================================================================

1.1 GREETING STANDARDS
Every customer must be acknowledged within 10 seconds of entering the store.
The standard greeting is warm, genuine, and personalized where possible:
- Use the customer's name if they are a registered Starbucks Rewards member.
- Make eye contact and smile before speaking.
- Do not recite a scripted greeting — keep it natural and conversational.
- Example: "Hi there! Welcome in. What can we get started for you today?"

Partners must avoid generic, robotic greetings such as "Next!" or "Can I help you?"
All partners are expected to maintain a positive, upbeat tone throughout the interaction,
even during high-volume periods.

1.2 SERVICE SPEED STANDARDS
- Drive-Thru: Target order completion within 3 minutes 30 seconds.
- Cafe Walk-In: Beverages should be ready within 4 minutes of order placement.
- Mobile Orders: Must be ready within 7 minutes of the order being placed in the app.
During rush periods (7-9 AM, 12-1 PM), managers may authorize additional staffing at bar.

1.3 PERSONALIZATION
Partners are encouraged to remember regulars' orders and preferences. The POS system
stores customer order history accessible via Starbucks Rewards ID. Partners should:
- Ask about customizations proactively: "Would you like your usual oat milk today?"
- Note dietary restrictions flagged on the customer's profile.
- Write the correct name on the cup — confirm spelling if uncertain.
- Offer a complimentary size upgrade on a customer's birthday (Rewards members only).

================================================================================
SECTION 2: COMPLAINT RESOLUTION PROCESS
================================================================================

All customer complaints must be handled with empathy, urgency, and professionalism.
The five-step resolution process must be followed in sequence:

STEP 1 — LISTEN WITHOUT INTERRUPTING
Allow the customer to fully explain their issue before responding.
Do not become defensive or dismiss the concern.
Use active listening signals: nodding, "I understand," "I hear you."

STEP 2 — ACKNOWLEDGE AND APOLOGIZE
Offer a genuine apology regardless of fault.
Example: "I'm so sorry that happened — that's absolutely not the experience we want
you to have here."
Do not use conditional apologies such as "I'm sorry you feel that way."

STEP 3 — CLARIFY THE ISSUE
Ask one or two focused questions to confirm you understand the problem fully.
Example: "Can you tell me what you ordered? I want to make sure I get this right for you."

STEP 4 — RESOLVE IMMEDIATELY
Within your authority, offer the most appropriate resolution:
- Remake the beverage or food item at no charge.
- Offer a complimentary item of equal or lesser value.
- Issue a Starbucks Rewards credit (requires manager PIN for amounts over $10).
- Process a refund per the Refund Policy guidelines.

STEP 5 — FOLLOW UP BEFORE THE CUSTOMER LEAVES
Before the customer leaves, check in: "Is everything better now? Are you happy with how
we resolved this?" If the issue remains unresolved, escalate to the shift supervisor
immediately. Do not let a dissatisfied customer leave without a supervisor involvement.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: CUSTOMER RECOVERY GUIDELINES
================================================================================

3.1 EMPOWERMENT AUTHORITY
All partners (not just supervisors) are empowered to remake a beverage or food item
once per visit per customer without manager approval. This is a fundamental service
commitment that requires no justification or documentation.

3.2 RECOVERY TOOLS AVAILABLE TO PARTNERS
- Complimentary beverage remake (any size, any customization).
- Complimentary pastry or food item under $5.
- Starbucks Rewards Star credit (up to 50 Stars) — manager PIN required.
- Digital feedback card directing customer to Starbucks.com/feedback.

3.3 RECOVERY TOOLS AVAILABLE TO SUPERVISORS AND MANAGERS
- Full refund (cash, credit, or Rewards credit) for any amount.
- Complimentary Gold Card loaded with up to $10 credit.
- Replacement of entire order for party orders under $50.
- Escalation to district manager for incidents involving safety or injury.

3.4 DOCUMENTATION
All recovery actions costing over $10 must be logged in the Daily Recovery Log
with: timestamp, customer description (not full name), issue type, resolution, and
partner initials. These logs are reviewed weekly by the store manager.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: LOYALTY PROGRAM POLICIES
================================================================================

4.1 STARBUCKS REWARDS OVERVIEW
Stars earned: 2 Stars per $1 spent on qualifying purchases (Starbucks app or card).
Non-qualifying: alcohol, bottled beverages purchased for resale, taxes, tips.

4.2 REDEMPTION TIERS
- 25 Stars: Customization (extra shot, pump of syrup, splash of milk)
- 50 Stars: Brewed hot coffee, bakery item, or packaged snack
- 150 Stars: Handcrafted drink, hot breakfast item, or parfait
- 200 Stars: Lunch sandwich, protein box, or salad
- 400 Stars: Select merchandise or packaged coffee (up to $20 value)

4.3 STAR EXPIRATION
Stars expire 6 months from the date they were earned if no qualifying purchase is made.
Partners may not extend star expiration — customers must contact Starbucks customer
support at 1-800-STARBUC (782-7282) for any star adjustment requests.

4.4 BIRTHDAY REWARD
Active Starbucks Rewards members receive one free beverage or food item on their
birthday. The reward is valid for the entire birthday calendar month. Partners must
scan the customer's app to apply the reward — manual overrides are not permitted.

4.5 PARTNER DISCOUNT ON REWARDS
Partners using their Starbucks partner number receive a 30% discount. This cannot
be combined with Stars redemption on the same transaction.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS
================================================================================

5.1 ACCESSIBILITY
All Starbucks locations must comply with ADA requirements. Partners must:
- Offer to carry orders to accessible seating areas if requested.
- Read menu items aloud upon request without hesitation or annoyance.
- Accept assistance animals without question.
- Ensure the mobile order pickup shelf is accessible to wheelchair users.

5.2 DIETARY RESTRICTIONS
Partners must clearly communicate ingredients and allergen information when asked.
When in doubt, direct the customer to the posted allergen guide or the Starbucks app.

Do NOT make guarantees about cross-contamination — our kitchens are not allergen-free
environments. Use the exact language: "While we take precautions, I cannot guarantee
our products are completely free from any specific allergen."

5.3 SEVERE ALLERGY PROTOCOLS
If a customer declares a severe allergy (peanuts, tree nuts, gluten, dairy):
1. Alert the shift supervisor immediately.
2. Use a clean, separate blender if preparing a blended beverage.
3. Use new, unopened milk containers.
4. Have the customer review the full ingredient list before preparing.
5. Flag the cup with a red allergen sticker.

================================================================================
SECTION 6: DIGITAL ORDER ISSUES
================================================================================

6.1 MISSING MOBILE ORDERS
If a customer claims a mobile order is missing:
- Check the mobile order queue first (may still be in queue or completed).
- Ask for the order name and confirmation number from the app.
- If not found after 5 minutes from order time: remake at no charge immediately.
- Report the lost order in the Mobile Order Tracker log with order ID.

6.2 WRONG ITEMS IN MOBILE ORDER
- Offer immediate remake of the correct item.
- Do not ask the customer to prove the order was wrong.
- If the item is out of stock, offer a full refund or comparable substitute.

6.3 APP PAYMENT FAILURES
If a customer's app payment fails at pickup:
- Do not withhold the completed order — release it and take alternate payment.
- Have the customer contact Starbucks support (1-800-STARBUC) for billing issues.
- Log the incident: order ID, date, time, and approximate order value.
