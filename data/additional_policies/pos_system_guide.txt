STARBUCKS POS SYSTEM GUIDE — ORACLE SIMPHONY
Version 2.8 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Applies To: All Partners Authorized to Operate the Register

================================================================================
SECTION 1: OPENING THE REGISTER
================================================================================

1.1 STARTING YOUR SESSION
1. Power on the POS terminal if it is off — press the power button on the back panel.
2. Wait for the Oracle Simphony login screen to appear (approximately 60 seconds).
3. Enter your 4-digit Partner PIN. Never share your PIN — each transaction is logged
   against the PIN that processed it.
4. Select your register assignment from the Till Management screen.
5. Count the starting cash in the till — verify against the printed Starting Till Report.
   Standard starting till: $200 (4 x $20, 10 x $10, 20 x $5, 30 x $1, coin roll set).
6. If the till does not match the Starting Till Report, notify the shift supervisor
   before processing any transactions.

1.2 TILL ASSIGNMENT
Each partner is assigned to a specific till at the start of each shift. Do not process
transactions on another partner's till without a supervisor till transfer authorization.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: PROCESSING DIFFERENT PAYMENT TYPES
================================================================================

2.1 CASH PAYMENTS
1. Enter the order total and press [TENDER].
2. Select [CASH] on the payment screen.
3. Count the cash received from the customer in full view of the customer.
4. Enter the amount tendered — the system will calculate change automatically.
5. Count change back to the customer in descending denomination order.
6. Place the customer's cash on the till ledge until the transaction closes —
   never put cash in the drawer before giving change.
7. Press [CLOSE] to finalize the transaction and print the receipt.

2.2 CREDIT/DEBIT CARD
1. Enter the order total and press [TENDER].
2. Select [CREDIT/DEBIT].
3. Direct the customer to tap, insert, or swipe their card on the PIN pad.
4. For chip transactions: do not remove the card until the POS displays "Remove Card."
5. For transactions over $50: the system will prompt for a signature on the PIN pad.
6. Confirm the approved transaction and offer a receipt.
Note: Never manually key in card numbers — if the card cannot read, ask for alternate payment.

2.3 STARBUCKS MOBILE APP AND REWARDS
1. Press [TENDER] > [STARBUCKS APP].
2. Ask the customer to open their Starbucks app and present the barcode/QR code.
3. Hold the barcode scanner over the code until it beeps.
4. The system will deduct the balance or apply the Stars redemption automatically.
5. If the scan fails after 3 attempts: have the customer open the manual barcode
   (bottom of the app payment screen) and enter the 16-digit code manually.
6. Stars are credited to the customer's account within 24 hours of the transaction.

2.4 STARBUCKS GIFT CARDS
1. Press [TENDER] > [GIFT CARD].
2. Swipe or scan the physical gift card, or scan the digital card in the app.
3. The POS will display the current balance on the card.
4. If the balance is insufficient: the system will prompt for a split tender.
   Select [SPLIT] and process the remaining balance on any alternate payment method.
5. To check a gift card balance without a purchase: press [GIFT CARD] > [BALANCE INQUIRY].

2.5 CORPORATE ACCOUNTS AND PURCHASE ORDERS
Corporate accounts must be pre-approved and registered in the system.
1. Press [TENDER] > [CORPORATE].
2. Select the corporate account from the dropdown list.
3. Enter the employee's corporate ID or purchase order number.
4. The system will verify the account and apply the corporate discount if applicable.
5. A receipt will print — give the original to the customer for expense reporting.

2.6 SPLIT PAYMENTS
To split a payment across two methods:
1. Press [TENDER] > [SPLIT TENDER].
2. Enter the amount to be charged to the first payment method.
3. Process the first payment type.
4. The system will prompt for the remaining balance on a second method.
5. Process the second payment type to close the transaction.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: APPLYING DISCOUNTS AND PROMOTIONS
================================================================================

3.1 PARTNER DISCOUNT
1. Before tendering, press [DISCOUNT] > [PARTNER DISCOUNT].
2. The system will prompt for the partner's 8-digit partner number.
3. Enter the partner number — the 30% discount applies automatically.
4. Partner discount cannot be combined with Stars redemption in the same transaction.

3.2 PROMOTIONAL DISCOUNTS (HAPPY HOUR, BOGO)
Promotions are loaded into the POS automatically — no manual entry needed.
If a promotion is not applying automatically:
1. Confirm the date and time of the promotion match the current transaction.
2. Verify the qualifying items are in the cart.
3. Press [PROMO OVERRIDE] — enter your manager PIN — and manually apply the promotion code.
4. Log all manual promotion overrides in the Discount Override Log.

3.3 MILITARY AND FIRST RESPONDER DISCOUNTS
1. Verify identification (badge, military ID) — do not skip this step.
2. Press [DISCOUNT] > [MILITARY/FIRST RESPONDER].
3. The 10% discount applies to all menu items for the individual.
4. Note: discount does not apply to bottled items or merchandise.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: HANDLING VOIDS AND REFUNDS IN POS
================================================================================

4.1 VOIDING A TRANSACTION (SAME SESSION)
A void cancels a transaction before the end of the business day:
1. Press [MANAGER FUNCTIONS] — enter manager PIN.
2. Select [VOID TRANSACTION].
3. Enter the transaction number from the receipt or swipe the customer's payment card.
4. Confirm the void — the original payment method will be reversed automatically.
5. Cash voids require cash to be physically returned to the customer from the till.
6. Log all voids in the Void Log with reason code.

4.2 PROCESSING A REFUND (DIFFERENT SESSION OR PRIOR DAY)
Refunds after the business day are closed require manager authorization:
1. Press [MANAGER FUNCTIONS] — enter manager PIN.
2. Select [REFUND].
3. Enter the original transaction number (from the customer's receipt) or search by date.
4. Select the items to refund — partial refunds are supported.
5. Choose the refund method:
   - [ORIGINAL METHOD]: Refunds to the card or account used at purchase.
   - [CASH]: Only for transactions under $20 — requires manager approval for over $20.
   - [GIFT CARD]: Issue a new Starbucks gift card loaded with the refund amount.
6. Confirm the refund — a refund receipt will print automatically.
7. Document in the Refund Log: transaction number, amount, reason, approver PIN.

4.3 PARTIAL REFUNDS
Partial refunds follow the same steps as full refunds but select only the specific
items being refunded. Common use cases:
- Customer received wrong size and prefers a price adjustment.
- One item in a multi-item order was incorrect.

4.4 REFUND AUTHORITY LIMITS
- Partners: May process refunds up to $25 using their own PIN (requires manager approval).
- Shift Supervisors: Up to $50 without additional authorization.
- Store Managers: Unlimited refund authority within the system.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: END OF DAY RECONCILIATION
================================================================================

5.1 RUNNING THE END-OF-DAY REPORT
At closing (after the last transaction):
1. Press [MANAGER FUNCTIONS] > [END OF DAY].
2. Print the Z-Report (full daily summary — prints once and cannot be reprinted).
3. Review: Total sales, payment method breakdown, void summary, discount summary.

5.2 TILL COUNT
1. Remove the till from the register and carry it to the back office.
2. Count all denominations separately using the counting tray.
3. Enter each denomination into the Till Count Sheet.
4. The system will calculate the expected total vs. actual.
5. Variance under $1.00: Acceptable — log and proceed.
6. Variance $1.01-$10.00: Investigate — check void log and discount log.
7. Variance over $10.00: Mandatory incident report — notify district manager.

5.3 SAFE DROP PROCEDURE
All cash above the $300 safe-balance threshold must be dropped into the safe:
1. Count and band bills in $100 bundles.
2. Complete the Safe Drop Envelope with date, amount, partner initials.
3. Seal the envelope and deposit through the safe drop slot.
4. Log the drop in the Cash Management Log immediately.

================================================================================
SECTION 6: COMMON ERROR CODES AND SOLUTIONS
================================================================================

ERROR 1001 — NETWORK TIMEOUT
Cause: POS lost connection to the payment processor.
Solution: Wait 30 seconds and retry. If persistent, switch to offline mode by pressing
[OFFLINE] and process card transactions manually with the imprinter. Contact IT at ext. 5100.

ERROR 2003 — CARD DECLINED
Cause: Customer's card was declined by their bank.
Solution: Ask the customer to use an alternate payment method. Do not reveal the
reason for decline — it is between the customer and their bank.

ERROR 3007 — GIFT CARD BALANCE INSUFFICIENT
Cause: Gift card does not have enough balance for the transaction.
Solution: Split the tender. Press [SPLIT] and charge the remainder to another method.

ERROR 4012 — LOYALTY ACCOUNT NOT FOUND
Cause: The Starbucks Rewards barcode could not be matched to an account.
Solution: Ask the customer to refresh their app and try again. If still failing, complete
the transaction without Stars credit and advise the customer to contact Rewards support.

ERROR 5500 — PRINTER PAPER OUT
Cause: Receipt printer paper roll is empty.
Solution: Open the printer cover on the right of the terminal. Remove the empty spool.
Insert a new 80mm thermal paper roll (stored under counter, right side). Close the cover
and press [TEST PRINT] to confirm alignment before processing next transaction.

ERROR 9999 — SYSTEM FAULT
Cause: Unrecoverable POS system error.
Solution: Note the full error message displayed. Do not attempt to resolve independently.
Call POS Support immediately: 1-888-XXX-XXXX. Switch to manual order pad and cash-only
operations until the system is restored. A manager must authorize all manual transactions.
