STARBUCKS REFUND AND ORDER CORRECTION POLICY
Version 3.2 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Confidential — For Internal Store Operations Use Only

================================================================================
SECTION 1: ELIGIBLE REFUND CONDITIONS
================================================================================

Starbucks is committed to delivering the highest quality products and customer
experience. Refunds or order corrections may be issued under the following
approved conditions:

1.1 WRONG ORDER RECEIVED
    A customer received a product that does not match what was ordered (e.g.,
    wrong drink size, wrong beverage type, incorrect food item). Full refund or
    remake is authorized. No additional approval required for amounts under $50.

1.2 QUALITY ISSUE
    The product did not meet Starbucks quality standards at the time of
    delivery or pickup. Examples include:
    - Beverage served at incorrect temperature (cold hot drink, warm cold drink)
    - Visibly incorrect preparation (e.g., missing shots, wrong milk)
    - Food item past freshness date or visibly spoiled
    - Foreign object found in product
    A full refund or immediate remake must be offered. Quality issues must be
    reported within 24 hours of purchase.

1.3 DUPLICATE CHARGE
    Customer was charged more than once for the same order due to system error
    or cashier mistake. Full refund of duplicate amount is mandatory and must
    be processed within 1 business day of report.

1.4 ALLERGIC REACTION / INCORRECT INGREDIENTS
    Customer received an item containing allergens not disclosed or present in
    the order (e.g., dairy in a non-dairy order, nut product). Full refund is
    authorized immediately. An incident report must also be filed within 2
    hours. Store manager must be notified regardless of order amount.

================================================================================
SECTION 2: REFUND AMOUNT THRESHOLDS AND APPROVALS
================================================================================

Refund authority is tiered based on transaction amount to ensure appropriate
oversight and financial control.

2.1 UNDER $50.00 — STORE MANAGER APPROVAL
    - Authorized by: Store Manager or Shift Supervisor (designated)
    - Process: Can be approved and processed on the spot via POS system
    - Documentation: Standard refund log entry required
    - Notification: No district notification required

2.2 $50.00 TO $100.00 — DISTRICT MANAGER NOTIFICATION REQUIRED
    - Authorized by: Store Manager
    - Process: Manager approves but must notify District Manager within 4 hours
    - Documentation: Refund log + brief written justification submitted to DM
    - Notification: District Manager email/message notification mandatory
    - AI Systems: Human-in-the-loop (HITL) approval required before processing

2.3 ABOVE $100.00 — DISTRICT MANAGER APPROVAL REQUIRED
    - Authorized by: District Manager only
    - Process: Store Manager submits refund request; DM must approve before
      any funds are returned to customer
    - Documentation: Full incident report + itemized receipt + customer ID
    - Notification: Regional Operations team copied on all approvals
    - Timeline: DM must respond within 2 business hours; customer informed
      of pending review
    - AI Systems: AI may not approve or recommend approval; human decision only

================================================================================
SECTION 3: TIME WINDOW RESTRICTIONS
================================================================================

3.1 STANDARD REFUND WINDOW — 7 CALENDAR DAYS
    Applies to: Wrong order, duplicate charge, defective product
    Customers must report the issue within 7 days of the original transaction
    date. Requests beyond this window will not be processed without Regional
    Manager exception approval.

3.2 QUALITY ISSUE WINDOW — 24 HOURS
    Applies to: Temperature issues, preparation quality complaints
    Due to the perishable nature of beverages and food, quality complaints must
    be submitted within 24 hours of purchase. Complaints received after this
    window will be assessed on a case-by-case basis by the Store Manager.

3.3 CHANGED MIND / BUYER'S REMORSE — 30 MINUTES
    Applies to: Customer who changes their mind after receiving the order
    Only eligible for refund if reported within 30 minutes of order fulfillment.
    Product must be unused and in original condition where possible.
    Changed-mind refunds for amounts above $50.00 are subject to District
    Manager review and are not guaranteed.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: NON-REFUNDABLE SCENARIOS
================================================================================

The following situations do not qualify for a refund under standard policy:

4.1 Promotional or discounted items marked as "Final Sale"
4.2 Digital gift card purchases once the card has been activated and used
4.3 Mobile orders that have been fully prepared and are awaiting pickup for
    more than 30 minutes (customer no-show)
4.4 Customization errors made by the customer (e.g., ordered wrong size,
    selected wrong milk type in app) — a courtesy remake may be offered
    at manager discretion
4.5 Orders placed through third-party delivery platforms (Uber Eats, DoorDash)
    — these must be handled through the respective platform's refund process
4.6 Bulk/catering orders cancelled after preparation has begun
4.7 Requests submitted beyond applicable time windows without exception approval
4.8 Loyalty Stars and reward redemptions — Stars are non-refundable once used

================================================================================
SECTION 5: STEP-BY-STEP REFUND PROCESS
================================================================================

STEP 1 — ACKNOWLEDGE THE CUSTOMER
    Listen to the customer's concern without interruption. Thank them for
    bringing it to your attention. Do not argue or dispute the claim initially.

STEP 2 — VERIFY ORDER DETAILS
    Pull up the transaction in the POS system using the order number, loyalty ID,
    or payment method. Confirm order date, items, and amount paid.

STEP 3 — CONFIRM ELIGIBILITY
    Cross-reference the reason for refund against Section 1 (eligible conditions)
    and Section 3 (time windows). If ineligible, explain the policy politely and
    offer a goodwill gesture (e.g., complimentary drink coupon) if appropriate.

STEP 4 — DETERMINE APPROVAL LEVEL
    Check the order total against Section 2 thresholds:
    - Under $50: Proceed with manager/supervisor approval
    - $50–$100: Notify District Manager before or immediately after processing
    - Over $100: Pause and contact District Manager for pre-approval

STEP 5 — PROCESS THE REFUND
    Issue refund to the original payment method:
    - Credit/Debit Card: 3–5 business days for funds to appear
    - Mobile Pay: 1–3 business days
    - Gift Card: Reloaded immediately
    - Cash: Issued from the register with supervisor sign-off

STEP 6 — LOG THE TRANSACTION
    Enter all refund details in the Refund Log system immediately after
    processing. Required fields: order ID, customer name, refund amount,
    reason code, approving manager, timestamp.

STEP 7 — FOLLOW UP
    For refunds above $50, a follow-up satisfaction message should be sent to
    the customer within 24 hours confirming the refund has been processed.
